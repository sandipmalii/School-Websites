About Us

Department of Computer Science and Application was established with the objective of imparting quality education in the field of Computer Science. The Department offers two undergraduate programmes viz. BCA and B. Sc Computer Science which commenced in the year 2010 and 2013 respectively.

To broaden the knowledge of the students in the emerging technologies, the department frequently conducts seminars and invited talks with the support of experienced resource persons from the industry. Project work is a part of the curriculum. The department has to keep abreast of the new technologies and applications and keep the students updated. The department is well equipped.

E-SIA

The Esia Study application developed by the students of Safi Institute of Advanced Study, which provide an easier way of education for both students and teachers. The app consist of many features that provide a better platform for learning and teaching. Esia Study application is developed in a way to improve the method of learning. The application is loaded with many features which provide a great way to learn and teach. As the application has a lots of features which will help the students and teachers to get the required materials for the education purpose. The features include:

Syllabus

The application provides the most updated syllabus that are able to view and download by the students and teachers .The app produce the most recent syllabus of both under graduate and post graduate provided by the university. The students are able to view and download It by selecting the specified departments and semester, the syllabus is available in both descriptive and pdf formats.

Previous question papers

The previous year’s question papers are one of the most important part in the mode of education. Esia Study app provides many previous year’s question papers which the students can access by selecting their departments and year, the application allows them to both view and download it.

Sias classroom

The online class room is a great platform for learning purpose. Both the students and teachers have access to it, where the teachers and students has to login to enter to it. In this platform the teachers are able to share any type of study materials including audio, video, pdf, ppt etc. by sharing them on the platform.

Sias Publications

The college publication includes the releasing of college magazines and other projects of college. The notifications are available here to get the updates of upcoming events that are going to take place in the college. So the user can view the notifications and details from it. This platform act as as a college bulletin where we could find relevant information of the activities that take place in the college.

Magazine

In this platform we are able to view the magazines released by the college. Any user is able to read and view it.

Timetable

The Esia Study application provide the day-by-day time table of all the departments in the college including both undergraduate and post graduate courses. In the time table we are able to access the subjects and teachers of the particular departments. And also the ongoing class.
